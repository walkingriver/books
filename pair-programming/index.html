What Changed in Two Years

In 2023, I wrote the first edition of this book. ChatGPT was new. Copilot was impressive. AI pair programming felt like the future.

I was completely wrong about how fast that future would arrive.

By early 2026, I shipped 49 pull requests in Q1—over 95,000 lines of code. Three epics in 6 days that would have taken 8 weeks. Not because I got better at coding, but because I learned to configure AI tools properly.

This book is the completely rewritten second edition—full of real stories, honest mistakes, and the workflows that actually work when you stop treating AI like autocomplete and start treating it like a pair programming partner.
